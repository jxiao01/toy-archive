Toy archive

Toy Archive is a shared collection of toys, organized by when and where they were found. Each entry is a file: add your own, click to inspect, drag to rearrange.

Behind each toy is a place and a moment. The archive pulls live weather from that location and searches Wikipedia for context about the toy or the year it was acquired, turning personal objects into something a little more collective.
